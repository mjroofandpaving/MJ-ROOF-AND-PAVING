About Us

Since its establishment in 2018, M&J Roof and Paving High Pressure and Maintenance Services (Pty Ltd) has been a trusted provider of construction and maintenance solutions.

Our professional services encompass high-pressure washing, roof and house maintenance, waterproofing, spray painting, renovations, paving, building, gutter installations, epoxy flooring, swimming pool construction

and maintenance, oil stain removal, road sign repainting, and handyman services.

With a commitment to excellence, our skilled team ensures the highest standards in every project we undertake. Whether it's rejuvenating surfaces, enhancing the durability of floors, or constructing and maintaining swimming pools. We bring expertise and dedication to every task.

Our comprehensive range of services reflects our dedication to meeting diverse construction and maintenance needs.

At M&J Roof and Paving, we pride ourselves on building lasting relationships with clients through trust

reliability, and exceptional results. Choose us for your construction and maintenance requirements, and experience the professionalism and quality that define our company.
